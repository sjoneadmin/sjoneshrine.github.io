Supporting Future Leaders

A specialized program for young women ages 18-25 entering adulthood without guidance or support. Your journey begins here.

Start Your Application Learn More

Our Mission

At Sjòne Shrine, we believe that every young woman deserves the tools, community, and guidance to build a life of purpose and stability. We provide intensive, holistic support for those navigating the transition to adulthood without traditional family or institutional backing.

Comprehensive Education

Life skills, financial literacy, career development, and personal growth curriculum designed for real-world success.

Strong Community

A supportive network of peers, mentors, and staff who understand your journey and are committed to your success.

Proven Results

Our graduates leave with practical skills, emotional resilience, and a clear path forward in their chosen fields.

Our Four-Year Journey

Designed like a story unfolding in chapters, our program provides structured progression through four transformative years.

1

Year One: Foundation

Orientation & Stabilization

Establishing safety, routine, and core life practices. Focus on basic financial literacy, health and wellness, communication skills, and building healthy relationships.

  • • Personal safety and emergency preparedness
  • • Basic financial management and budgeting
  • • Health and nutrition foundations
  • • Communication and conflict resolution
2

Year Two: Expansion

Skill-Building & Growth

Advanced skill development, digital literacy, and self-governance. Participants begin to specialize in areas of interest while building independence.

  • • Advanced financial planning and investing
  • • Digital skills and online presence management
  • • Career exploration and skill development
  • • Self-advocacy and personal boundaries
3

Year Three: Leadership

Responsibility & Contribution

Taking on leadership roles, contributing to the community, and developing creative output. Participants mentor newer members and lead projects.

  • • Mentoring and peer support leadership
  • • Project management and team coordination
  • • Creative expression and portfolio development
  • • Community engagement and service learning
4

Year Four: Transition

Sovereignty & Independence

Designing your next path and preparing to leave with stability. Focus on career placement, housing preparation, and building lasting support networks.

  • • Career placement and professional development
  • • Independent housing and living arrangements
  • • Building lasting personal and professional networks
  • • Legacy planning and giving back to the community
